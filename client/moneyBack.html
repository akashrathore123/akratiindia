Money Back
Any claims for defects, damages, or shortages must be made in writing within two (2) business days after receipt of the merchandise. We will exchange only those orders that were processed incorrectly. The replacement of the order will take place only after the receipt of returned merchandise. We will not exchange/take return of the order if it is determined by us that the order was misused or mishandle by the buyer or part of the order was used by the buyer and part of the order is being returned. Return shipping amount will be paid by the customer and the amount will be same that was paid by the customer to receive the product.

1- Customer can ask for money back if there is any defect in the product received by them.

2- Amount will be transferred to the customer bank account that is provided by them in the returning form.

3- NO refund will be provided if the product seems to be used or mishandled by the customer.

4- Return shipping amount will be paid by the customer, and we will deduct that amount from the money back.

5- As in clause 4- , the return shipping amount will be same that was paid to get the product.

6- Customer has to request for money back within 2 days from the day on delivery.

7- Money will be transferred in the bank account of customer within 2 working days after receiving the product by us to get are satisfied that the product has not been used or mishandled by the customer.

8- Order under the category of “BULK ORDER” are not eligible for return or refund.
