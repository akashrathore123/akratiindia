Money Back
1- Customer can ask for money back if there is any defect in the product received by them.

2- Amount will be transferred to the customer bank account that is provided by them in the returning form.

3- NO refund will be provided if the product seems to be used or mishandled by the customer.

4- Return shipping amount will be paid by the customer, and we will deduct that amount from the money back.

5- As in clause 4 , the return shipping amount will be same that was paid to get the product.

6- Customer has to request for money back within 2 days from the day on delivery.

7- Money will be transferred in the bank account of customer within 7 days after request of customer.

8- There is no money back guarantee for “BULK ORDER”.
